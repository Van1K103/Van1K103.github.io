Gửi cô,

Happy Teachers' Day!
To the guiding stars in our lives,
You light the way where dreams arise.
With wisdom deep and hearts so true,
You shape the world in all you do.
Through patient hands and caring hearts,
You teach us how to play our parts.
Each lesson shared, each story told,
A treasure more than gems or gold.
Today we pause, with gratitude say,
Thank you, dear teachers, in every way.
May joy and peace your hearts fulfill,
For all you give with love and skill.
